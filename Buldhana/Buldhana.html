BULDHANA

INFORMATION

Buldhana is a district in Maharashtra State of India. It has a total of 13 Talukas in this district. The district has an total area of 9,661 sq km. There are 12 towns and 1,444 villages in this district. Buldhana is one of districts of Maharashtra in India, Buldhana District population in 2021 is 2,767,419 (estimates as per aadhar uidai.gov.in Dec 2020 data). As per 2011 census of India, Buldhana District has a population of 2,586,258 in 2011 out of which 1,337,560 are male and 1,248,698 are female. Literate people are 1,879,874 out of 1,048,892 are male and 830,982 are female. People living in Buldhana District depend on multiple skills, total workers are 1,219,641 out of which men are 734,024 and women are 485,617. Total 363,983 Cultivators are depended on agriculture farming out of 223,649 are cultivated by men and 140,334 are women. 537,970 people works in agricultural land as labor, men are 289,944 and 248,026 are women. Buldhana District sex ratio is 934 females per 1000 of males. Next Buldhana District Census will be in 2021. The district has an total area of 9,661 sq km., 121 sq km is urban and 9540 sq km is rural. Out of total population of Buldhana, 2,870,747 in the district, 548,860 are in urban area and 2,037,398 are in rural area. 110,220 households are in urban, 451,284 are in rural area. 429,116 literate people are in urban, 1,450,758 are in rural area. The most spoken Language in Buldhana is Marathi.
